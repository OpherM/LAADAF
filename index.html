What We Do

Partnership

We partner with national and international organisations in creating awareness on the evil effects of drug abuse from primary,secondary to tertiary level of education

Awareness Programmes

We carry out programs to raise the awareness of drug abuse and its effets amongst the young and innocent children

Networking

We aim to establish a network of free drug club in local schools and internationally where drug education lectures would be given

Talks and Seminars

We aim to engage in radio,television talks and seminars on evil effect of drug abuse cases

Who We Are

We are a Non-Profit organisation with the aim of complementing government agencies afforts on drug abuse, advocacy and eradication to the young ones right from their primary school.
