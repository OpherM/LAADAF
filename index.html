What We Do

Partnership

We partner with national and international organisations in creating awareness on the evil effects of drug abuse from primary,secondary to tertiary level of education

Awareness Programmes

We carry out programs to raise the awareness of drug abuse and its effets amongst the young and innocent children thus preventing the desire for consumption.

Networking

We aim to establish a network of free drug clubs in local schools where drug education lectures would be given

Talks and Seminars

We aim to engage in radio,television talks and seminars on evil effects of drug abuse cases

Who We Are

We are a Non-Profit organisation with the aim of complementing government agencies afforts on drug abuse, advocacy and eradication to the young ones right from their primary school.

Our Proprietor

Dr. Folaranmi Modupe Olowoleni (Nee Babatolu), a native of Takete-Isao, Yagba-East Local Government Area of Kogi State, was born in Lokoja, Kogi State on April 19, 1961.
Dr. Olowoleni was appointed Registrar and Secretary to the Council of the University of Ilorin on April 28, 2018. She is the second female Registrar in the history of the University and the first with a Doctor of Philosophy Degree.
Dr. Olowoleni is a member of many professional bodies, including the Association of University Administrators (AUA), Manchester, United Kingdom; The Nigerian Institute of Management (NIM); The Association of Nigerian University Professional Administrators (ANUPA); and a member of the Association of Registrars of Nigerian Universities (ARUN).
